## 📱 How to Rank on Social Media (What YOU Need to Do)

To rank on social media platforms, you need to:

### Instagram:
- Post 3-5 times per week
- Use 5-10 hashtags per post (relevant to your niche)
- Write captions with keywords
- Engage with followers (reply to comments)
- Use Instagram Reels
- Add location tags

### TikTok:
- Post at least once per day
- Use trending sounds
- Add keywords to your captions
- Use 3-5 hashtags
- Engage in the first hour after posting
- Create content that answers questions

### LinkedIn:
- Complete your profile 100%
- Post articles or updates 3x per week
- Use industry keywords in your posts
- Engage with other people's content
- Join groups and participate

### Facebook:
- Post regularly (3-5x per week)
- Use local keywords if you're a local business
- Engage with comments
- Use Facebook Stories
- Join relevant groups

### YouTube:
- Upload videos consistently (at least 1-2 per week)
- Use keyword-rich titles
- Write detailed video descriptions
- Add 10-15 tags per video
- Create custom thumbnails
