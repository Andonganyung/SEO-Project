4. **Establishes professional presence**
   - Looks credible and trustworthy
   - Mobile-friendly
   - Fast and optimized

### ❌ What This Does NOT Do:

1. **Does NOT automatically rank you on social media**
   - Instagram ranking = you need to post regularly

   - *SEO helps Google find you, not social algorithms*

2. **Does NOT replace social media posting**
   - You still need to post 3-5x per week
   - Use hashtags relevant to your industry
   - Engage with followers
   - *We build the foundation, you create content*

3. **Does NOT guarantee instant results**
   - SEO takes 1-3 months to show results
   - Requires patience and consistency
   - Organic growth compounds over time
   - *This is an investment, not a quick fix*
